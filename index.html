Why Join

Simple & Social

Partner-based doubles only. Weekly matches that fit real life, with standings and a fun finals night.

Free Courts, Pro Feel

We leverage Augusta’s best public courts and supply balls, structure, and a consistent schedule.

Budget Friendly

Keep costs low while still enjoying quality play, medals, and professional photos for your team.

How It Works

  • Season length: 6–8 weeks (1 match night per week)
  • Divisions: Beginner & Intermediate
  • Match format: to 11 win by 2, or best-of-3 depending on turnout
  • Locations: Greater Augusta free public courts
  • Finals night with medals and photos

FAQ
Do I need a partner to register?
Yes — the league is partner-based doubles only. Please register as a team of two.
What’s the cost?
We keep fees affordable. Final pricing is listed on the registration flow; sponsors help us keep costs low.
Where are matches played?
Free public courts around Greater Augusta (e.g., Riverview Park, Warren Road, Lonnie Morris). Final schedule is shared after registration closes.
What should I bring?
Your paddle, water, good vibes — we provide balls and your team wristbands in your chosen color.
