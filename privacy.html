Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. The Augusta Pickleball Club only collects the information you provide during registration or when contacting us (such as your name, email, and phone number). This information is used solely for league communication, scheduling, and updates.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Payments are processed securely through third-party providers such as PayPal, CashApp, or Stripe. We do not store your payment card details.

If you have any questions about your data, please contact us at hello@augustapickleball.club.
